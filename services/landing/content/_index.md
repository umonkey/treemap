## Data Collection

The platform provides a streamlined workflow for gathering accurate data on urban vegetation directly from the field. These tools focus on precision mapping, visual documentation, and efficient data handling to ensure high-quality data collection in any urban environment.

<div class="feature-grid">
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-map-location-dot"></i>
        <h2>Interactive Mapping</h2>
        <p>Tools for precise geolocation and visualization, including features to quickly add rows of trees and manage draft entries.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-camera"></i>
        <h2>Visual Documentation</h2>
        <p>Support for photo updates to track health and growth over time.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-cloud-arrow-up"></i>
        <h2>Optimized Data Usage</h2>
        <p>Support for background photo uploads when on Wi-Fi, preserving battery and mobile data while working in the field.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-bookmark"></i>
        <h2>Tree Bookmarks</h2>
        <p>Ability to track specific trees of interest for easier follow-up and monitoring.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-layer-group"></i>
        <h2>Custom Imagery</h2>
        <p>Support for custom map tiles, enabling the use of high-resolution internal aerial imagery for precise local mapping.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-street-view"></i>
        <h2>Street View Integration</h2>
        <p>Support for 360° street-level imagery via Mapillary, including specialized tools to triangulate and verify tree positions directly from the imagery.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-mobile-screen"></i>
        <h2>Accessible Technology</h2>
        <p>Create accurate maps using standard consumer-grade devices. The platform is designed to produce professional results without requiring expensive surveying equipment.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-clipboard-check"></i>
        <h2>Efficient Survey Modes</h2>
        <p>Choose from various survey modes tailored to your available tools. The platform offers optimized workflows that adapt to your equipment and team size to maximize productivity in the field.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-mobile-button"></i>
        <h2>Mobile-First Collection</h2>
        <p>Use your existing smartphone to collect all data in the field through a specialized mobile interface, then switch to a laptop for in-depth data analysis and management.</p>
    </div>
</div>

## Management and Analysis

Beyond data entry, the platform offers robust tools for organizing and analyzing your urban forest database. From AI-assisted insights to global data exchange, these features support long-term environmental planning.

<div class="feature-grid">
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-magnifying-glass"></i>
        <h2>Powerful Search</h2>
        <p>Built-in search functionality to quickly find trees based on species, address, or any other collected attribute.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-robot"></i>
        <h2>AI & Agent Integration</h2>
        <p>Native support for the Model Context Protocol (MCP), enabling AI agents to perform complex queries, generate reports, and provide deep insights into your urban forest data.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-chart-pie"></i>
        <h2>Comprehensive Analytics</h2>
        <p>Detailed reporting including biodiversity indexing, species leaderboards, and street-level coverage statistics to help prioritize greening efforts and track environmental goals.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-earth-americas"></i>
        <h2>OpenStreetMap Integration</h2>
        <p>Bidirectional data exchange with OSM, allowing for pulling existing tree data and pushing local updates back to the global map.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-bolt-lightning"></i>
        <h2>Performance</h2>
        <p>A Rust-based backend capable of managing large datasets with minimal infrastructure requirements.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="feature-card">
        <i class="fa-solid fa-file-export"></i>
        <h2>Data Portability</h2>
        <p>Export your data in standard GeoJSON format for use in QGIS, ArcGIS, or custom web APIs. Your data remains accessible and compatible with industry-standard GIS tools.</p>
    </div>
</div>
